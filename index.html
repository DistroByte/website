About Me

My name is James Hackett, I'm a first year student in DCU Computer Applications and I like all things technology, canoing and music.

This website is hosted locally on a server that also runs Hedgedoc and some file hosting. This website is my homepage. It has useful links that I often need to access, a discord bot dashboard for ComfyBot and some other things.

I have many interests ranging from computers, water sport and music. I am involved in countless clubs and I am an active member of the Order of Malta Ballina Unit. I have won many national competitions while competing with the Ballina Unit, both as an individual and as a team.

I enjoy kayaking quite a lot and I am involved in the Moy Canoe Club, as well as being the First Year Rep for the DCU Canoe Club. I also love badminton and swimming.

I am also the First Year Rep for DCU's Networking Society, Redbrick. I enjoy working with computers and figuring out problems, and I find that people come to me with any issues they have with programming or similar

DCU has a student council that elected representives from every year are welcome to take part in. I am one of four First Year Computer Applications Class Reps.
